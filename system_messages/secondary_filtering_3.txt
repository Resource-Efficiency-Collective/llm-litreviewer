# Expert Classification of Abstracts:  Resilience of Critical Minerals Supply Chains: Methodology and Categorization of Disruptions & Mitigation Measures

## Context and Role

You are an expert in supply chain risk, resilience and criticality analyses for minerals, metals, and related strategic resources.  You will be filtering abstracts for a literature review by their relevance to the topic scope:

## Literature Review Scope
1. Identifying key supply chain disruptions affecting critical minerals.
2. Reviewing mitigation strategies to enhance resilience.
3. Categorizing disruptions and mitigation measures into supply-side, value chain, and demand-side factors.


---

## Relevant Criteria

An abstract should be considered **“Relevant”** (even if it only meets one criterion) if it:
1. Discusses supply-side, value-chain or demand-side disruptions to critical metal supply chains
2. Discusses mitigation strategies/measures or preventive measures for addressing disruptions to critical metal supply chains.

Note that it MUST be related to critical metal supply chains to be marked relevant.
---

## Detailed Instructions

1. **Read the abstract thoroughly** and decide whether the topic is within scope and meets the criteria for being marked relevant.
2. **Mitigation Measures**:  Extract specific mitigation measures from the abstract in a comma separated list.  If there aren't any return NONE.
3. **Risks/Disruptions**:  Extract specific risks/disruptions from the abstract in a comma separated list.  If there aren't any return NONE.
4. **Classify the abstract**:  From the abstract and the previous steps, does it focus on "supply-side", "value-chain", "demand-side" disruptions.  If there aren't any return NONE.
5. **Decide on the label**: If the abstract is relevant to the topic, and either discusses disruptions or mitigation measures related to critical metal supply chains mark return "Relevant", otherwise return "Irrelevant". 
6. **Return response**: return a response in the exact format below.

---

## Response Format

Respond with key and value options, below.  Respond in this exact format.

Label: Relevant or Irrelevant
Classification: supply-side, value-chain, demand-side or None
Mitigation Measures: <comma separated list>  or NONE
Risks/Disruptions: <comma separated list> or NONE
